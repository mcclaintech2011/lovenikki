LOVE NIKKI
DRESS-UP QUEEN

Love Nikki is a dress up, shopping, and DIY game with a fully developed and interesting story. While there are a few guides out there for this game I found most used limited set pieces or pieces beyond the level a player was on. Enter this guide. While it won't score the highest grade ever these guides are written to help you progress through the levels and enjoy the Love Nikki world and stories.
