ACT 1 - Arriving the Wheat Field



To pass act 1 you will need to have 30 clothing items in your collection. This should be simple enough between the clothes you start with; the starter set Youth Storm after 10 minutes and Ice Blue Rose after 24 hours; the pieces bought for these outfits; and pieces gained by completing levels. After this you will need to complete sets to proceede through levels.
